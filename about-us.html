We are 3 Girl Scouts, Emily, Gracie, and Evelyn, working to earn our Bronze Award. In order to earn it, we have to do a take action project, and we decided to help Colorado's animal habitats, specifically the grasslands. The adults that helped us are Molly, (Gracie's Mom) who helped plan, lead us, and provide recourses, Dave, (Evelyn's Dad) who also helped plan, lead us, and provide recourses, and Darron, (Emily's Dad) who helped create this website. We made posters to put in our school and made this website to alert and educate people on how to help.
